Available Space Calculator

Calculate the available storage space for your ZMS configuration.

Advanced mode: enter one pair per line in the format 0x01:010203. ID and data must be hexadecimal. Whitespace around the : separator is ignored (data may include spaces or underscores). Empty lines and lines starting with # are ignored.

Advanced mode: enter one pair per line in the format /data/key1:010203. Path is plain text and data must be hexadecimal. Whitespace around the : separator is ignored. Empty lines and lines starting with # are ignored.

Error: Total Storage Used exceeds the total available storage size. Increase the storage size or reduce the effective data set.

Warning: Effective data occupies more than 50% of the available storage size. The storage should be dimensioned so that effective data occupies less than 50% of available storage.
